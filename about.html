Who I am

I'm a UX/UI and Interaction Designer based in Zurich, currently at Hexagon Geosystems. My background is in computer science: I studied at Instituto Superior Técnico in Lisbon before doing a Master's in Interaction Design at the University of Sydney.

That combination shapes how I work. I understand how things are built, which means I can push design further without losing sight of what's feasible. It also means I sometimes just build things myself, like the MQTT communication layer for a public installation I designed during my master's.

I care about clarity. In design, in code, in how a product explains itself to the person using it. Less interested in novelty for its own sake, more interested in things that are quietly, reliably useful.
